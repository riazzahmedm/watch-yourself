# WatchYourself MVP Plan

## MVP Goal

Build the smallest version of WatchYourself that makes the product feel fundamentally different from a normal logging app.

## Primary User Promise

"WatchYourself helps me log what I watch, discover something that fits how I feel, and understand my taste over time."

## MVP Scope

### Must Have

- user accounts and profiles
- search/import titles from TMDb into local storage
- log movie watches
- log series watches at episode level
- add rating
- add short review
- mark rewatch
- select pre-watch mood
- select post-watch feeling
- simple watch history
- initial Taste DNA profile
- basic Movie Twin matching
- weekly and monthly reflection summaries

### Nice to Have After MVP

- followers and activity feed
- comments and likes
- yearly recap
- collaborative mood lists
- richer life journal entries
- push notifications tied to mood prompts

## Core MVP Screens

### 1. Onboarding

- choose a few favorite titles
- choose moods you commonly watch in
- optionally answer "what do you usually want from a watch?"

### 2. Home

- quick mood entry points
- continue watching
- twin activity snippets
- personal summary card

### 3. Search and Title Detail

- search title
- title metadata
- mood tags
- community mood fit
- log button

### 4. Log Flow

- watched on date
- movie or episode selection
- rating
- review
- rewatch toggle
- pre-watch mood
- post-watch feeling
- optional life note

### 5. Diary

- chronological logs
- filters for mood, rating, rewatch, format

### 6. Taste DNA

- top affinities
- behavioral dimensions
- recent shifts
- twin matches

### 7. Timeline

- week summary
- month summary
- reflective prompts

## MVP Success Metrics

### Activation

- first log created within first session
- onboarding completed

### Retention

- 7-day repeat logging rate
- users returning to mood discovery
- users opening timeline summaries

### Differentiation

- percentage of logs containing mood context
- percentage of users viewing Taste DNA
- percentage of users visiting a Movie Twin profile

## Suggested Build Order

1. auth and user profile
2. catalog ingestion and title pages
3. logging flow
4. diary/history
5. mood discovery
6. Taste DNA computation
7. Movie Twin matching
8. timeline summaries

## Product Rules

- logging must be faster than writing a full review
- mood discovery must work even with sparse user history
- Taste DNA must be explainable, not magic
- timeline summaries should feel reflective, not robotic
